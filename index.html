Drive Safely at Night – Say No to High Beams in Traffic

Why High Beams Hurt

High beams are great for dark, empty roads, but in traffic, they can blind oncoming drivers, causing discomfort and even accidents. A single flash of bright light can reduce visibility for seconds – enough time for a mishap. Let's keep our roads safe by using low beams in busy areas.
